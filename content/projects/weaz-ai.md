  - Weaz AI is a premium, zero-barrier AI platform that combines chat
    assistance, document intelligence, and productivity tools into one unified
    experience.
  - Users can upload PDFs, spreadsheets, presentations, code files, and images
    to inspect and query them inside a built-in document viewer.
  - A persistent cognitive backend logs interests to customize response vectors,
    while Projects & Study workspaces help users organize notes, assignments,
    and study decks.
  - The Resume & Career Center evaluates ATS match percentages, critiques
    sections, and drafts cover letters to support job seekers.
